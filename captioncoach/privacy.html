Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Sun Mistry LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates Caption Coach AI. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Caption Coach AI stores the following data locally on your device only using encrypted on-device storage:

  • Your name and profile picture (optional)
  • Your selected niche, tone, and voice samples
  • Captions you have generated (your Folio history)

We do not transmit this data to our servers. We do not have access to it.

2. AI Processing

When you generate a caption, your topic, niche, tone, and voice samples are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to produce the result. This data is processed by Anthropic under their privacy policy. We do not store or log this data on our end.

3. Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

4. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers in this App.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete the App at any time to remove all associated data. There is no account to delete.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the date at the top of this page when we do.
